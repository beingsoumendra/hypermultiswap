XP system (preview)

Every swap earns XP that counts for seasonal rewards. Example rules:

  • Base Rate

    2 XP per $10 swapped (rounded).

  • Referral Bonus

    5% additional XP to the referrer when their friend swaps.

  • Streaks & Events

    Extra XP for consecutive days and limited-time events.

Roadmap

A focused path from BSC aggregator to a full multi-chain protocol and HYSP launch.

  1. Phase 1 — BSC Aggregator

    Launch HyperMultiSwap v1 on BNB with swap aggregation, audited routing and XP tracking.

  2. Phase 2 — Multi-chain Core

    Add Base, Ethereum and other EVM chains with unified routing.

  3. Phase 3 — Liquidity & HyperDEX

    Introduce liquidity layer and HyperDEX v1.

  4. Phase 4 — Partnerships & IDO

    Integrations, partnerships, and IDO preparation.

  5. Phase 5 — Token Launch, Staking & Rewards

    TGE, staged distributions, staking and ongoing seasonal rewards.

    Launch governance, proposals and DAO treasury utilities.

  8. Phase 8 — Ecosystem Scale & Interop

    SDKs, bridges, enterprise integrations and scaling programs.
