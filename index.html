What we’re building

HyperMultiSwap will route swaps across the best liquidity on BNB and other EVM chains. Every swap earns on-chain XP for future HYSP airdrops and rewards.

BSC Aggregator

Phase 1 launches on BNB with 1inch under the hood, low fees and a gaming-style UI.

Multi-chain Engine

Expand routing to Base, Ethereum and more EVM chains with a unified experience.

XP & Quests

Swaps, referrals and streaks earn XP instantly, tracked by wallet address for future seasons.

Roadmap

A focused path from BSC aggregator to a full multi-chain protocol and HYSP launch.

  1. Phase 1 — BSC Aggregator

    Launch HyperMultiSwap v1 on BNB with swap aggregation, 0.23% fee routing and XP system.

  2. Phase 2 — Multi-chain Aggregator

    Add Base, Ethereum and additional EVM chains with a unified routing engine.

  3. Phase 3 — Liquidity & HyperDEX

    Introduce liquidity layer, optimized routes and the first version of HyperDEX.

  4. Phase 4 — Partnerships & IDO

    Strategic integrations, ecosystem partners and IDO preparation for HYSP.

  5. Phase 5 — TGE & Airdrop

    Token Generation Event, HYSP staking and Season 1 airdrop based on XP and volume.

HYSP Token (coming)

HYSP will be the native token of the HyperMultiSwap ecosystem — powering fee share, staking, governance and XP boosts. Planned supply: 10M with fair allocation to early users.
