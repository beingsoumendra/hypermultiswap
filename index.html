What we’re building

HyperMultiSwap will route swaps across the best liquidity on BNB and other EVM chains. Every swap earns on-chain XP for future HYSP airdrops and rewards.

BSC Aggregator

Phase 1 launches on BNB with 1inch under the hood, low fees and a gaming-style UI.

Multi-chain Engine

Expand routing to Base, Ethereum and more EVM chains with a unified experience.

XP & Quests

Swaps, referrals and streaks earn XP instantly, tracked by wallet address for future seasons.

Roadmap

From BSC aggregator to a full multi-chain protocol — staged milestones and community-first incentives.

  1. Phase 1 — BSC Aggregator

    Launch HyperMultiSwap v1 on BNB with swap aggregation, 0.23% commission and XP system.

  2. Phase 2 — Multi-chain Core

    Extend routing to Base, Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism and additional EVM chains.

  3. Phase 3 — Liquidity Layer & HyperDEX

    Introduce liquidity vaults, smart routing and HyperDEX for optimized swaps and reduced slippage.

  4. Phase 4 — Partnerships & Integrations

    Wallet integrations, bridge partners and ecosystem integrations to scale liquidity and reach.

  5. Phase 5 — HYSP TGE & Staking

    Token Generation Event, staking, governance preparation and utility unlocks for early users.

  6. Phase 6 — Listings, Liquidity Boost & Market Growth

    DEX/CEX listings, liquidity mining programs and global marketing to grow volume.

  7. Phase 7 — Governance, DAO & Ecosystem Scale

    Launch governance, DAO treasury utilities, developer SDKs and cross-chain tooling.

  8. Milestone — Season 2 Airdrop Trigger

    When HyperMultiSwap reaches $500,000,000 cumulative swap volume, Season 2 Airdrop will be activated — XP-weighted rewards for active users and referrers.
